Wave Medium Analysis

Visualizing v = fλ. Observe how identical frequency pitches stretch into longer wavelengths when sound enters a faster medium like water.

Critical Insight

Frequency (f) is determined by the source and remains constant. Because sound travels faster in water, the waves stretch out to cover more distance per cycle.
