The Calculation

An echo represents a round trip. If the person hears the echo after 1 second, the sound took 0.5 seconds to reach the cliff and 0.5 seconds to return.

v = 343 m/s // Speed of sound
ttotal = 1.0 s // Total round trip
tone-way = 1.0 / 2 = 0.5 s
d = v × tone-way
d = 343 × 0.5
d = 171.5 m
0.0s: Sound Emitted
0.5s: Hits the Cliff
1.0s: Echo Heard
