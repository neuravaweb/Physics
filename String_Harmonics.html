Critical Insight

The wave speed (422.4 m/s) on a string is determined by its physical properties (tension and linear mass density), not how you pluck it.

Change the harmonic above! Notice how Wavelength decreases and Frequency increases proportionately, perfectly canceling out so the wave speed v remains constant.
