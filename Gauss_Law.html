Gauss's Law: Electric Flux

A +2 C positive point charge resides at the center of a transparent Gaussian sphere (radius = 1m). The electric field \(\mathbf{E}\) radiates uniformly outward. As the field lines pierce the surface, they create electric flux \(\Phi_E\), visualized by the bright flashes at intersections.

$$ \Phi_E = \oint_{S} \mathbf{E} \cdot d\mathbf{A} = \frac{Q_{\text{enc}}}{\varepsilon_0} $$
